FRIENDS

About Friends

Friends is an American television sitcom, which aired on NBC from September 22, 1994, to May 6, 2004, lasting ten seasons. The show revolves around six friends in their 20s and 30s who live in Manhattan, New York City.

Monica Geller, Chandler Bing, Rachel Green,Ross Geller, Joey Tribbiani, Phoebe Buffay
